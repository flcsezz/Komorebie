# Design System: Luminous Glass

## Brand Direction
- Deep, cinematic, modern, and restrained
- Premium sci-fi atmosphere without gamer clutter

## Color Foundation
- Obsidian: `#0A0A0A`
- Glass base: `rgba(255,255,255,0.08)` to `rgba(255,255,255,0.14)`
- Cyan accent: `#00F2FF`
- Electric purple accent: `#9D00FF`
- Use luminous accents sparingly against dark neutrals

## Typography
- Headers: Outfit
- Body: Inter
- Numeric display: use tabular numerals when possible for timer stability

## Material Language
- Glass panels need depth, edge light, and layered blur
- Depth should come from lighting, shadow, scale, and parallax
- Bloom and glow are accents, not the whole interface

## Motion
- Follow spring-based, expressive transitions
- Prefer smooth state transitions over continuous idle animation
- Focus mode reduces motion amplitude and frequency

## 3D Guidelines
- 3D should anchor atmosphere and state, not obstruct tasks
- Camera movement must be subtle during active study
- Environmental reactivity should be slow and calming

## Accessibility Constraints
- Preserve readable contrast on all glass surfaces
- Do not rely on glow alone to communicate state
- Support reduced motion equivalents for all major animations
